Ring Of Honor Results (1/4/24): Kyle Fletcher Defends Against Willie Mack

Publish date: 2024-06-09

This week’s episode of Ring of Honor Wrestling on HonorClub kicks off with a video package from AEW Worlds End highlighting Eddie Kingston winning the Continental Classic and his comments following the match.

  • Griff Garrison hits Serpentico with a Torture Rack Bomb to secure the pinfall victory.
  • Lance Archer squashes JP Harlow in quick order with a Blackout to win the match via pinfall.
  • Dalton Castle hits Peter Avalon with the Bangarang to score the pinfall victory.

Ethan Page and Scorpio Sky are backstage. Scorpio Sky says Page is the most talented man on the Ring of Honor roster, and he’s one of his best friends. Sky says Page was there to support him when he was TNT Champion, and now he’s here to support Page while he’s on his journey to capture Ring of Honor Gold.

Page says he’s the best version of himself he’s ever been, and he’s ready for anything and everything. Page says he trusts Sky with his life and says this is the year of the Men of the Year.

  • Queen Aminata locks Maya World in the Juicy Lock to secure the win via submission.
  • Shane Taylor hits the Marcus Garvey Driver on one of The Boys to secure the pinfall victory for his team.

The tag team rivalry isn’t done in Ring of Honor

Griff Garrison and Cole Karter are backstage. Karter says they are dedicating Garrison’s win to Maria Kanellis but Garrison didn’t seem on board with that. They are jumped by Serpentico and Angelico and left lying.

  • Red Velvet hits Alex Gracia with The Mix to score the pinfall victory.
  • Diamante scores the pinfall victory on Rachael Ellering to pick up the win for her team.
  • Kyle Fletcher hits Willie Mack with a piledriver to score the pinfall victory.

Dalton Castle is backstage. Castle says he’s exhausted and that he hasn’t slept since Final Battle because he’s worried about the state of Ring of Honor. Castle says Johnny TV costing him the World Television Championship has taken everything from him.

Johnny TV and Taya Valkyrie show up and mock Castle. Castle tells them to shut up and says they took everything from him. Johnny TV says they’ll take even more. Castle says he wants to smash Johnny’s face. Taya says he will not do that and makes out with her husband before they both walk away.

  • Kommander counters Gringo Loco and rolls him up for a flash-pin victory for his team.

El Hijo del Vikingo, Lee Johnson, and Komander celebrate as Ring of Honor goes off the air.
