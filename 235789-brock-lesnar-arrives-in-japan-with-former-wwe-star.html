Brock Lesnar Arrives In Japan; With Former WWE Star

Publish date: 2024-06-25


Credit: PWInsider.Com

Former WWE Champion Brock Lesnar arrived in Tokyo, Japan this morning as he his scheduled for an appearance at the New Japan Pro Wrestling Tokyo Dome event tomorrow. Lesnar didn’t travel alone, former WWE star Rena “Sable” Mero, who he is engaged to, also made the trip abroad with him.

Lesnar told the Japanese media that he is interested in fighting for New Japan, K-1, and PRIDE but won’t do anything but “watch” until his contract with WWE expires in March. Lesnar stated that he “may do something” tomorrow at the Dome.
