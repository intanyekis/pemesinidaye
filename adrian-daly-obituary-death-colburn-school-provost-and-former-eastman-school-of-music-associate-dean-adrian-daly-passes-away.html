Adrian Daly Obituary – Death: Colburn School Provost and Former Eastman School of Music Associate Dean, Adrian Daly Passes Away

Adrian Daly Obituary: Dr. Adrian Daly, a former member of the Glee Club, died suddenly. On August 10, 2023, his death was declared. Many people who knew him during the course of his well-lived life mourn his passing, leaving a gaping hole in their hearts.

Who is Adrian Daly?

The University of Notre Dame awarded Adrian a Master of Arts in Music Performance and Literature in 1991. He obtained a Doctor of Musical Arts in Piano from the Eastman School of Music two years later, in 1994.

Adrian later served in a variety of capacities at the Eastman School of Music, including that of Associate Dean for Admissions.

Adrian moved on to occupy the position of Provost at the Colburn School in 2015 after being appointed as Dean of the Conservatory at the Cleveland Institute of Music in 2009. Those who knew him will sadly miss this real Irishman!

A heart attack was the cause of Adrian Daly’s death. When the blood supply to the heart is obstructed or diminished, a heart attack occurs.

Typically, a blockage in one of the blood vessels that supplies the heart with oxygen causes this to occur. Without adequate blood and oxygen, the heart cannot function properly, which can result in serious issues or even death.
