Getting To Know About Angle Coulby

Publish date: 2024-06-22

Angel Coulby is widely known as an English small-screen actress in films, television, who came to fame for being the cast as Guinevere ‘Gwen’ in the BBC fantasy television series "Merlin". 

She was born on August 30, 1980, in Islington, London, England, UK. Her nationality is British and holds the birth sign of Virgo.

Early Life And Family Status

Angel was born in Islington, London, England, UK, and raised in North London which is one of the most developed countries at the present time. She was raised with love and care by her parents.

Furthermore, her parent's information has been disclosed publically yet. It seems that she is the only child of her parents because she has not shared any information regarding her siblings.

Education

The actress of Merlin is well educated and finished her early education while she was shifted to Edinburg to study acting.

After that, she got a college degree in acting from Queen Margaret University. At the age of 20, her first achievement was rewarded with the Laurence Olivier Bursary in 2000  at university. 

Career Highlights

Angel Coulby first appeared in her on-screen career in a  TV mini-series named "Orrible" as Shiv in 2001.

From 2002 to date, she has been cast in various TV series like "Casualty"  as Sally, "Having It Off" as Kylie, "As If" as Amber, "Talk to Me" as faith/talk-show assistant, "Vincent" as Gillian Lafferty, "Doctor Who" as Katherine, "Merlin" as Gwen, and many more.  

Moreover, she has appeared as a guest in various TV mini-series like Conviction, Dancing on the Edge, Undercover, and Innocent. 

She has been portrayed in two movies as “A Good Theif” as Leah Pickering in 2002 and "Secret Life" as Young Mum in 2007.
