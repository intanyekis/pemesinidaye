Coachella 2019 Celeb Festival Style: Best, Wildest Fashion Looks

Publish date: 2024-05-26

Festival season is officially here! Celebs descended on Palm Springs over the weekend for part two of Coachella 2019 — and they did so in style. In addition to seeing headliner Ariana Grande slay with Justin Bieber (in case you missed it, she reunited ’NSync — sans Justin Timberlake — on stage on Sunday, April 14), stars showed off their best boho style in crop tops, denim cutoffs, minidresses and more.

Before Ari (rocking her signature sky-high ponytail and thigh-high boots) and the boyband hit the stage, performers like Kacey Musgraves, Ella Mai and H.E.R. entertained the star-studded crowd that included models-of-moment like Kendall Jenner, Gigi Hadid, Kaia Gerber, Olivia Culpo and more.

Oh, and then there was the case of Cardi B surprising everyone on Saturday, April 13, by inviting Selena Gomez on stage to perform. The “Back to You” singer was festival season fashion #goals in a ruffled all-white-everything ensemble, complete with piled on necklaces and mile-long braids.
