David Sacks- Biography

David Sacks was born in South Africa on May 25, 1972. Before founding and serving as CEO of a social networking service called Yammer, Inc., this successful entrepreneur headed PayPal and established the genealogical research website Geni.com. He and PayPal founder and fellow entrepreneur Peter Thiel attended Stanford University at the same time and co-wrote a work titled The Diversity Myth: Multiculturalism and the Politics of Intolerance at Stanford. After graduating from Stanford University with a degree in economics and subsequently earning a law degree from the University of Chicago, he worked briefly as a consultant for McKinsey & Company.
