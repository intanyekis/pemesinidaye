Enzo Amore Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Bio:
Enzo Amore (born Eric Arndt on December 8, 1986 in Hackensack, New Jersey) is an American professional wrestler. He started his athletic career as a football player in high school, then at Division III college, Salisbury University. Enzo was a longtime wrestling fan and made a video of himself showing off some moves, which his trainer gave to WWE star, Triple H, who worked out at the same gym as him. Triple H helped Enzo get a tryout with the WWE, and he made his NXT debut in April 2013. Enzo teamed up with Colin Cassady in Aug. 2013, and they formed an epic Tag Team, winning the Tag Team of the Year Award at the NXT Year-End Awards in 2015. The guys made the main WWE roster in April 2016.

Best Known For:
Enzo is best known as a wrestler for the WWE.

Personal Life:
Enzo was fighting for the Tag Team Championship at WWE Payback on May 1, when he was thrown out of the ring, suffering a neck injury. He was carted off on a stretcher, but was released from the hospital just a few hours later, diagnosed with a concussion. He has a degree in journalism from Salisbury University.
