Gerard Butler is best known as a Scottish actor in the entertainment industry. He has appeared in various films and television series over the years, including ‘Mrs Brown’ (1997), the James Bond film ‘Tomorrow Never Dies’ (1997), and ‘Tale of the Mummy’ (1998). He also starred as Count Dracula in the popular 2000 film ‘Dracula 2000’ and made lasting impressions in other roles on ‘Attila,’ Lara Croft: Tomb Raider – The Cradle of Life,’ and the 2004 version of ‘The Phantom of the Opera.’

Gerard’s work has been recognized throughout the years in the form of award nominations. Among them, he has won an MTV Movie + TV Award, a World Stunt Award, and a Golden Camera Award.
