The Cast Of Little People, Big World Ranked By Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Highlights

  • Matt Roloff's estimated net worth mainly comes from his farm and other business ventures.
  • Amy Roloff's net worth was earned through farm ownership, book sales, and recent marriage.
  • Zach Roloff's net worth is $700,000, accrued through his role on the show, coaching, and family decisions.

When Little People, Big World first premiered in 2006, most people, including its producers, probably didn't see it reaching the kind of numbers it did and is still doing. The show follows the lives of the Roloffs, a family who owns a farm close to Portland, Oregon and follows them as they navigate new challenges that come their way.

The hit show has come a long way since its beginning and so have all the cast members. While some members of the family have fallen off the radar, others are still constantly making headlines, such as Zach and his family. In general, owning a farm implies that loads of money is probably coming your way regularly, and that was the case of the Roloffs. That, added to money made from the show, has made the cast members significantly well-to-do.

Here are Little People, Big World cast members ranked by their net worth.

Updated February 2024: There have not been any changes to the net worths of any of the cast of Little People, Big World. However, there will be big changes to Amy Roloff's net worth at some point down the line, after Matt Roloff announced that he was going to be leaving the farm to her when he dies. Zach Roloff and his wife, Tori, announced that they are exiting the series, a show that Zach has been on for 25 seasons.

7 Matt Roloff

Net Worth: $6 Million

  • Matt will be appearing in Season 25 of Little People, Big World

Apart from the fact that he ran a successful farm with his ex-wife Amy, Matt Roloff is also credited as a business entrepreneur, author, and television personality. A large part of Matt’s money came from his farm, which led him to explore other business ventures (most of which were initially tied to his ex-wife), but things changed after their relationship crumbled.

Shortly after Matt and Amy officially got divorced, Matt and a formal farm official, Caryn Chandler, confirmed that they were dating via social media. As expected, this raised a lot of eyebrows, but they did indeed have a serious relationship as they are now engaged.

Currently, Matt has an estimated net worth of $6 million.

Raising even more eyebrows, Matt made the announcement that took everyone by surprise in February 2024, which was that he was leaving the farm to Amy after he dies.

6 Amy Roloff

Net Worth: $6 Million

  • Amy will be appearing in Season 25 of Little People, Big World

After realizing her marriage with Matt Roloff wasn't working out, the two went their separate ways. Both Matt and Amy had joint ownership of a lot of things while they were married, one of which included the Roloff farmland. After the divorce proceedings, Amy decided to move out of the farmhouse and sell a portion of her stake to Matt to focus more on herself. However, she still has stakes in the property and earns money from it.

Additionally, she also earns money from the books she has authored, one of which is her book Short and Simple Fall Recipes. Things are going pretty well for Amy right now as she recently found love again and got married to Chris Marek.

Amy is generally a successful woman and has her net worth at an estimated at $6 million.

5 Audrey Mirabella Botti

Net Worth: $1 Million

  • Audrey will not be appearing in Season 25 of Little People, Big World

Audrey is a well-known American blogger and television personality who rose to fame through her publications. Audrey has also appeared on television a few times besides Little People, Big World. She's married to Jeremy Roloff, and together they have three children. Apart from her time on-air and blogging, Audrey is also a designer and has been able to earn a huge amount of money from her talents.

The star has a current net worth of over $1 million.

4 Zach Roloff

Net Worth: $700,000

  • Zach will not be appearing in Season 25 of Little People, Big World

Like all the other Roloff kids, Zach Roloff had been earning profits from his role in the show early in his life; to be specific, since he was 14. While growing up, he had to help his parents out at the farm and his served as a tour guide, which earned him quite a chunk of money.

Now, apart from being a reality TV star, Zach also coaches youth soccer professionally and married a woman named Tori, a photographer. Together they have two kids, Jackson and Lilah. Zach and his family have gone through a lot this year and made a lot of big decisions, one of which includes his relocation to Washington.

Zach and Tori announced in February 2024 that they were exiting Little People, Big World. Season 25 will be their last season.

The reality star is currently estimated to be worth $700,000.

3 Jeremy Roloff

Net Worth: $700,000

  • Jeremy will not be in Season 25 of Little People, Big World

Jeremy is the first son of Matt and Amy Roloff and was part of Little People, Big World until he and his wife, Audrey, got married and left in 2014. Jeremy has been estranged from his family for years.

While he made a significant amount from his time on the show, the star also ventured into other lines of business, all of which have added up to his impressive net worth of $700,000

2 Jacob Roloff

Net Worth: $700,000

  • Jacob will not be in Season 25 of Little People, Big World

It seems that the members of the Roloff family have a lot more in common than we thought. Even though Jacob Roloff has stepped away from reality TV for some time now, he has found his way back to peoples’ screens.

Jacob is a YouTuber who has been filming his journey all over America with his wife, Isabel Rock. His channel has over seven thousand subscribers, and in addition to that, he is also a published author, just like his parents. Jacob also has a large social media following and occasionally uses it for promotions and advertisements.

He is currently estimated to be worth $700,000.

1 Molly Roloff

Net Worth: $700,000

  • Molly will not be in Season 25 of Little People, Big World

Molly Roloff is the only daughter of the Roloff family and has been away from the spotlight for some time now. Molly first left the show in 2012, when she went to college and obtained a Bachelor of Arts in Accounting, Literature, and Spanish Language. She then went on to land several jobs in the accounting line.

Though the Roloff family has made quite a lot of money from their entrepreneurship endeavors and fame, Molly decided that she wanted to forge her own path to success, and based on how she's doing now, it's safe to say she's on the right track. She had also made several guest appearances on the show over the years.

Presently, the 29-year-old has an estimated net worth of $700,000.
