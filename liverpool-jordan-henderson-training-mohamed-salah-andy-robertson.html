Publish date: 2024-07-22

Liverpool skipper Jordan Henderson wowed Andy Robertson and Mohamed Salah with his ripped physique as the players regrouped for pre-season training.

The 33-year-old is in fantastic shape ahead of the new campaign, with his teammates left in awe of his chiselled body.

Henderson recently shared images of him channelling his inner-Anthony Joshua by taking part in a vigorous boxing session.

And Robertson and Salah were left blown away by his new muscular physique when the Liverpool squad reported for their first day of pre-season on Tuesday at their Kirkby training centre.

As a shirtless Henderson was getting checked over by a Liverpool staff member, Robertson and Salah couldn't help but notice the England international's abdominal muscles.

In a clip shared by the Reds on Twitter, a gobsmacked Salah, who was also showing off his own toned physique, said to Robertson: "Did you see this? My god! Look at this!"
