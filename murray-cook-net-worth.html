Murray Cook Net Worth: $20 Million

Murray Cook, born on June 30th, 1960 in Cowra, New South Wales, is an Australian vocalist, songwriter, and guitarist who has a net worth of $20 million. He is best known as one of the founding members of the children’s band The Wiggles and is a regular guest on the Australian TV show Spicks and Specks.

Before joining The Wiggles, Cook played the guitar in the late ’80s bands Transistor and Finger Guns and Bang Shang a Lang in Sydney. He returned to college when things didn’t work out in the music field for him and studied early childhood education at Macquarie University, where he met Anthony Field and Greg Page and formed The Wiggles.

Cook is a member of The Wiggles and wears a red shirt while serving as an instrumentalist and vocalist. His children Georgia and Hamish can be seen in several Wiggles videos, and he owns a collection of thirty vintage guitars.

In 2008, Cook was named “the sixth best guitarist in Australia,” and he was made a Member of the Order of Australia in 2010 for service to the arts.
