Who is Nathalie Emmanuel?

England-born Nathalie Emmanuel is a film actress.

Presently, she is also famous for starring in films like “Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials”, “Furious 7”, and others.

Similarly, she is also popular for starring in the TV series, “Game of Thrones”.

She is portraying the character of ‘Missandei’ in the series since 2013.

Additionally, Nathalie is also a popular TV actress. As an actress, she is active in this occupation since 2006.

Nathalie Emmanuel: Early life, Childhood, and Education

Nathalie was born in Southend-On-Sea, Essex in England.

She was born Nathalie Joanne Emmanuel on March 2, 1989. Her mother is Dominican, and her father is half saint-Lucian and half English.

She had a passion for drama and art from a very young age.

Discussing her education, she attended independent St Hilda’s School. Afterward, she joined Westcliff High School for Girls.

Nathalie Emmanuel: Career, Salary, Net Worth

Nathalie Emmanuel’s career started at the age of 10. She starred in the West End production of the musical “The Lion King”.

She played the character of ‘Young Nala’ in the show. Discussing her TV career, she debuted in 2006 in a soap opera, “Hollyoaks”.

She portrayed the role of Sasha Valentine in it from 2006 to 2010.

Her portrayal of Missandei in “Game of Thrones” from 2013 to the present made her extremely famous.

Similarly, she made her debut in film in 2012 in the film, “Twenty8k”.

Afterward, in 2015, she starred in two major films, “Furious 7”, and “Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials”.

Then in 2017 and 2018, she reprised her role in the next installment of “Fast and the Furious” and “Maze Runner”.
