Noah Emmerich parents: Meet Andr Emmerich and Constance Marantz

Publish date: 2024-09-06

The Walking Dead actor Noah Emmerich was born into a Jewish family by his parents, André Emmerich and Constance Marantz.

Table of Contents

  • Andre Emmerich
  • Who Is Noah Emmerich?

Constance Marantz

Noah’s mother, Constance, is a concert pianist. She is of Hungary and Romania descent.

Andre Emmerich

Noah’s father, André was an artist who owned an art gallery. Andre hailed from Germany, but had moved his family first to Amsterdam, Netherlands, and later to New York in the 1940s.

Andre passed away in 2007.

Andre and Constance had two other sons – Toby Emmerich, a screenwriter who formerly served as chairman of the Warner Bros. Pictures Group; and Adam Emmerich, a corporate lawyer who specializes in merger and acquisitions.

Who Is Noah Emmerich?

Noah Emmerich is an award-winning actor whose roles in many films and TV series has made him an icon in the film industry.

Noah has earned a Saturn Award nomination, a Screen Actors Guild Award nomination, a Critics’ Choice Award nomination, and has won only one Critics’ Choice Award in his career.

Noah’s early acting roles began in the early 1990s with films like Beautiful Girls, Cop Land, The Truman Show, Life and Frequency, Pride and Glory, and Beyond Borders.

The Yale graduate has appeared in movies like The Truman Show and Little Children, Beautiful Girls, Frequency, Miracle, Jane Got a Gun, Super 8, and The Good Nurse.

Noah has also starred in many TV series including NYPD Blue, Melrose Place, Monk, White Collar, The Walking Dead, The Spy, Space Force, Suspicion, and Dark Winds.

In all, Noah is most famous for appearing as FBI agent Stan Beeman in the critically acclaimed TV series The Americans, which aired from 2013 to 2018.

His role in the series had earned him a Critics’ Choice Award nomination and award.
