Parker Lipman Family Business

Publish date: 2024-05-25

Parker Lipman parents are Carol McLeroy and William "Bill" Lipman. The reality star is proud to have two dads.

After Parker's mom and dad divorced, Carol got married to Zach, whereas Bill married a woman named Michelle Lipman. Parker considers his two fathers to be best friends.

William Lipman

Parker Lipman dad William Lipman was born in July 1958; he is currently 65 years old. 

Lipman is a graduate of the Georgia Institute of Technology. He has a degree in Industrial Management. 

Based in Suwanee, Georgia, he was been working as the President of Elite Contractors Group LLC since January 2014. 

Carol McLeroy

Carol was born in June 1968; she is 55 years old as of 2023. 

McLeroy is available on Instagram as @mrschik3n but her account is set to private. From her marriage to Zach, Carol has welcomed a son and a daughter, Parker's half-siblings.

Zach McLeroy

Parker Lipman stepdad Zach McLeroy was born in Athens, Georgia, in August 1962. He is currently 60 years old. 

McLeroy started working from a young age. He was only 13 when he got his first job on a chicken farm. During his high school years, he began working in restaurants.

Despite having degrees in marketing and economics, Zach was more interested in the restaurant business. So, with the help of his childhood best friend Tony, Zach opened the first Zaxby's on a $28 thousand budget in 1990. The restaurant soon became famous for its delicious chicken dishes. 

Zach sold the company in 2020 but remains as its Chairman for the time being.
