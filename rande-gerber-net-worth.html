American entrepreneur and former model Rande Gerber has an estimated net worth of $100 million. He was born on April 27, 1962 and grew up in the Five Towns. He graduated from Hewlett High School in Long Island in 1980 and later attended the University of Arizona where he earned his B.A. degree in marketing.

Gerber began as a model before shifting focus to his own business. He now owns and operates Midnight Oil, a chain of bars and lounges. His two brothers, Scott and Kenny, are also involved in the business. Scott is also responsible forthe financial aspect of the company called The Gerber Group. Delta Airlines began serving a signature series designed by Gerber in May 2007.

Rande Gerber is best known as the husband of supermodel and businesswoman Cindy Crawford. They have been married since May 29, 1998 and they have two children, Presley Walker and Kaia Jordan. He has been featured in two reality programs about Hollywood. He is good friends with George Clooney and was present during the wedding of Pamela Anderson and Kid Rock.
