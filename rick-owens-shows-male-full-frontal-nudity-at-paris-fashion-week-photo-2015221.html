Rick Owens Shows Male Full-Frontal Nudity at Paris Fashion Week: Photo

Publish date: 2024-06-19

Definitely, NSFFW (not suitable for fashion week)! 

We hope there was an age requirement to attend Rick Owens’ Fall 2015-2016 menswear collection during Paris Fashion Week on Thursday, Jan. 22, because the designer literally went balls to the wall in pushing the envelope.

Much to the surprise of the unsuspecting editors and socialites in attendance, a few of the male models strutted down the runway wearing loose-fitting tunics that had a strategic opening around the mens’ nether region. That’s not to say the entire collection was blush-inducing: Owens also introduced more traditional pieces like peacoats and sweaters.
