Shenae Grimes News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-07-30

Name:Shenae Grimes

Hometown:Ontario, CN

Birthday:October 24, 1989

About

Shenae Grimes was born on October 24, 1989 in Ontario, Canada. She currently is playing the role of Annie Wilson on the CW hit 90210, which is the spin-off of Beverly Hills, 90210. Prior to this role, she played Darcy Edwards on Degrassi: The Next Generation and was a recurring guest star on Naturally, Sadie where she played Arden Allcott.

Grimes also played teenage Shania Twain in the television movie Shania: A Life In Eight Albums and also appeared in the television movie, Picture This, opposite Ashley Tisdale. Grimes recently appeared in a music video for rock band Our Lady Peace’s single “All You Did Was Save My Life,” which was filmed in April 2009.

She has been romantically linked to her 90210 costar Adam Gregory and fellow Canadian, Ethan Rapport-Cole. Grimes married model and musician Josh Beech outside of London on May 10, 2013.
