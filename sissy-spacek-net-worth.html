Award-winning singer and actress Sissy Spacek has a net worth of $15 million. This great actress has won many accolades through her many roles in movies and television films. She also has a great singing career and voice acting role in the movie “The Man With Two Brains.”

This prominent actress received international recognition for her role in 1973 in the film Badlands, and her role in the Horror film Carrie. This got her a first Academy Award nomination. She has since won the Academy Award for Best Actress in 1980, in addition to numerous Oscar nominations, the latest being in 2001 for her role in the film In The Bedroom.

Sissy Spacek has two music albums to her name, Coal Miner’s Daughter (Soundtrack) and Hangin’ Up My Heart, released in 1983. She subsequently received a Grammy Award nomination for her single, Coal Miner’s Daughter.

Born on December 25, 1949, she had her inspiration derived from the early death of her brother, which saw her move to New York to start a singing career after completing her high school. She is married to Jack Fisk with whom she has two daughters; Schuyler Fisk and Madison Fisk.
