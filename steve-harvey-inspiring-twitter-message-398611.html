Steve Harvey Shares Inspiring Message On Honing Your Gifts

Publish date: 2024-09-03

Steve Harvey has never shielded his insight from the masses. Whether it’s an introspective talk on his daytime show, or penning relationship advice books, the 58-year-old entertainer remains steadfast in sharing his thoughts on how to improve your lifestyle. Now, he’s looking to motivate you through the power of remaining true to your gifts.

In a short tweet that he sent out on Wednesday (Jan. 13), Harvey revealed that after each taping of Family Feud, he has a heart-to-heart with the studio audience. In this particular video, he talks about jumping into your career’s desires without thinking about what detractors might say.

“See God, when he created all of us, he gave every last one of us a gift at birth,” he said. “He never created a soul without endowing them with a gift. You just got to quit looking at gifts as running, jumping, sing and dance.”

The actor/comedian later told stories of how his closest friends were involved in domestic jobs like cutting grass or detailing cars, but turned their talents into six-figure companies.
