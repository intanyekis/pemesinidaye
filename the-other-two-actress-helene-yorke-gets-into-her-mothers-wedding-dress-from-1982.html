The Other Two actress Helene Yorke gets into her mothers wedding dress from 1982

Publish date: 2024-06-29

The Other Two actress Helene Yorke gets into her mother’s wedding dress from 1982 after giving it ‘fashion surgery’ from costume designer who has worked with Lady Gaga

By Sameer Suri For Dailymail.com

Published: 01:45 EST, 24 December 2021 | Updated: 01:45 EST, 24 December 2021


The Other Two actress Helene Yorke fired up her Insta Stories this week to show herself modeling her mother’s wedding dress.

Helene’s mother modeled the bridal gown in 1982 and it did need a bit of refurbishment before passing on to a new generation.

On her Insta Stories the actress dished that she subjected the dress to ‘surgery’ by costume designer Sam Bennett who has worked with Lady Gaga.

Refurbished: The Other Two actress Helene Yorke fired up her Insta Stories this week to show herself modeling her mother’s wedding dress

Helene snapped a mirror selfie in which she modeled the dress in its original incarnation before having it adjusted.

‘Here’s the sad moment I realized I could’ve EATEN my Mom in 1982,’ quipped the actress who has featured on such shows as Masters Of Sex.

Once the outfit was revamped Helene posed in the finished product in a glamorous snap in which Sam could be spotted in the background.

‘Sam performed fashun surgery and gave it new life. Thought it wasn’t possible,’ Helene said of the whole process. 

Before: Helene snapped a mirror selfie in which she modeled the dress in its original incarnation prior to having it adjusted

‘I love and I can’t believe someone that skilled and magical said yes to helping me,’ she gushed adding a heart emoji for good measure.

Helene modeled her mother’s wedding dress posed on a New York City sidewalk with her sizzling new husband Bary Dunn.

‘Oh and of course @jennyandersonphoto took these photos. I wore my Mom’s dress from 1982, lovingly brought back to life by the amazing @sammybaebee,’ she wrote. 

Smoldering: Helene modeled her mother’s wedding dress posed on a New York City sidewalk with her sizzling new husband Bary Dunn

Bary and Helene got married this September in Brooklyn, modeling a dress by Maison Rime Arodaky rather than her mother’s bridal gown.

Helene, whose dating history includes the celebrity chef Bobby Flay, co-stars on The Other Two with Drew Tarver.

The show, whose leads play adult siblings whose teenage brother becomes an internet superstar, began on Comedy Central before moving to HBO Max.
