French singer, model and actress Vanessa Paradis has an estimated net worth of $150 million. Born Vanessa Chantal Paradis on December 22, 1972 in Saint-Maur-des-Fossés, Val-de-Marne, France, she began her singing career at the age of eight, and she became a child star at fourteen with her successful single Joe le taxi.

Paradis has also ventured into acting, and has appeared in several movies such as Cafe de Flore, Heartbreaker and Noce Blanche. The latter earned her the 1990 Cesar Award for Most Promising Actress. After a year, she promoted the fragrance Coco for Chanel, with the advertisement shot by Paul Gode. She then went back to singing and concentrated on her career for the next five years.

In 1995, Vanessa appeared in Elisa which was directed by Jean Becker. It became a big success in France and was released internationally. Despite her achievement with the show, she decided to concentrate on her private life with Johnny Depp and their children. In the recent years, however, she has filled tabloids and newspapers thanks to her breakup with Depp after fourteen years of living together. She went back to continue her singing and acting career.
