Who Was Hunter Schafer Before Getting Cast On 'Euphoria'?

Publish date: 2024-05-27

Hunter Schafer has become iconic as transgender high school student Jules Vaughn in the hit HBO series Euphoria. The show followers a group of high schoolers as they navigate sex, drugs, and love in the dark coming-of-age drama that's known not to shy away from real hard-hitting issues. Controversially, Hunter's performance has yet to earn her a Primetime Emmy Awards nomination, but Hunter has definitely made a huge impact with her first acting job.

Jules Has Been A 'Challenging' First Role

Hunter's acting abilities have been tested to the limit as she has had to portray a transgender teen with a heartbreaking backstory and issues of her own.

In the very first episode, Jules is the new kid in town. As a child, she was admitted to a psychiatric hospital and transitioned when she was thirteen. And Jules seeks out violent sexual interactions because, as Hunter explained in a video for the Euphoria Unfiltered series, Jules is in constant search of affirmation and 'love'.

“Jules being transfeminine and having this relationship to womanhood, seeing women in her life being treated a certain way by men, and a certain dynamic playing out in almost every interaction she’s witnessed…" said Hunter when talking about her character.

"I think it goes back to something deep-rooted, like transitioning, wanted to be treated a certain way by a man in order to feel like a woman in this very binary vantage point.”

How Did Hunter Schafer Get Famous?

Hunter Schafer stumbled upon a casting call for transgender girls on Instagram while she was preparing for her real-life plan — to go to fashion school after a year of modeling.

With no experience needed and her modeling agency hiring an acting coach for her, Hunter Schafer went for it, and that's how she became Jules. But before Euphoria turned Hunter's world upside-down, Hunter was a rising star in the modeling industry.

Hunter has modeled for some huge names and been a part of some huge projects as a transgender model, from Calvin Klein to Prada, to being proudly part of the Marc Jacobs' #GratefulNotHateful campaign during its 2018 Pride celebration. Projects were rolling in — now Euphoria has her career soaring.

Thanks to Euphoria, Hunter Schafer is a name people are not going to forget. The actress is still modeling, and another wonderful thing to come from the show is her relationship; fans were delighted to discover that Schafer is dating her Euphoria co-star Dominic Fike, who plays Elliot in the show.

The Euphoria star also wrote an episode of the show and revealed that writing for Euphoria was a lifeline after experiencing "the worst depression" back in 2020, thanks to the pandemic.

Hunter Schafer Is An Inspiration To Fans

Hunter still continues to model, and wants to broaden her horizons when it comes to her acting career, and will audition for other roles as well as being Jules. She's also the global ambassador of cosmetics brand Shiseido. So in other words, she is super busy following her heart and living her best life!

Hunter Schafer has inspired so many fans, whether it's through her looks as fans copy her make-up, to inspiring works of art, and even inspiring some to be confident enough to be their authentic selves.

"[So] proud to be surrounded and inspired by so many wonderful ladies, especially want to thank artists like claire cottrill, sade, hunter schafer and fka twigs for being so authentically themselves," one fan tweeted, "and full of character, you're my light."

"Hunter Schafer is just so amazing she's such a mood," another fan said, sharing some fun and casual pictures of the Euphoria star.

Fans cannot get enough of her actress and model's Instagram either. Hunter recently posed in a Miu Miu mini skirt and was in Milano with her mother for Prada.

Fans are obsessed with Hunter Schafer and for good reason — as well as being a stunning model and a talented actress, Schafer has always been an LGBTQIA+ activist. In high school, Schafer was an outspoken activist and was listed amongst the "21 Under 21" list of Teen Vogue in 2017.

Schafer has continued to work hard and try to make a difference, no matter what passion she follows, and fans are delighted to watch her succeed in everything that she does, as they continue to be inspired by her perseverance and creativity. Fans look forward to seeing more of both Jules and Hunter in the future!
