Wiki Biography of Whitney Port

Whitney Eve Port, known as Whitney Port, was born on March 4, 1985, in Los Angeles, California, United States. She has gained popularity as a television personality, fashion designer, and author, all of which have significantly contributed to her wealth. Her career in the entertainment industry began in 2006 when she appeared in the main cast of the television series “The Hills” from 2006 to 2010. Additionally, she achieved success with her fashion line “Whitney Eve” and served as a judge on the show “Britain & Ireland’s Next Top Model” in 2012.

Net Worth: $3.5 Million

Whitney Port graduated from Crossroads School and the University of Southern California with a major in Gender studies.

Her role in the reality series “The Hills,” created by Adam DiVello, and her leading role in “The City,” also contributed significantly to her net worth. She further boosted her wealth through appearances in TV series such as “Entourage,” “America’s Next Top Model,” and “Anna & Kristina’s Beauty Call.” Additionally, she served as the executive producer of “Hollywood is Like High School with Money” in 2010. Further expanding her television career, she hosted the game show “Genuine Ken” in 2011 and was a judge on “Britain & Ireland’s Next Top Model” in 2012. Port also had a cameo role in the feature film “What to Expect When You’re Expecting” in 2012.

Branching out into fashion, Port launched her clothing line “Whitney Eve” in 2008 and worked for fashion icon Diana von Furstenburg. She also authored the book “True Whit: Designing a Life of Style, Beauty, and Fun” in 2011, and contributed to “Teen Vogue.”

In her personal life, Whitney Port has been engaged to her long-term boyfriend Tim Rosenman, the producer of the reality series “The City,” since 2012.
