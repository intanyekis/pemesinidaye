Who is N. S. Krishnan ? N. S. Krishnan Wikipedia                   

N. S. Krishnan Wikipedia:  N. S. Krishnan was born November 29, 1908, in Nagercoil, Tamil Nadu. N. S. Krishnan was an Indian actor, comedian, theatre artist, playback singer and writer. He was 114 years old. He was given the title of ‘Charlie Chaplin’ of Indian cinema by film cine-goers.  He was fascinated by drama, stage, and theater, he often used to visit a drama theater in Nagercoil.

He even sold drinking bottles and got a free pass to see the dramas in Nagercoil. Thus, he got an opportunity to become a stage actor. From a stage actor, he went on to become the unchallenged comedy king of Tamil cinema. Later, he even turned filmmaker and later a politician. His first film was ‘Menaka’ which was released in 1936 and was a hit. Later he went on to act in nearly 150 films in his career.
